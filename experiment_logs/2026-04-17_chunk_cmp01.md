## Conclusion (justify your final choice)
- Final chunk size and overlap: 900 / 120
- Why it works better for the 2025 budget PDF: The larger window preserves longer policy/fiscal statements, which improves retrieval of complete budget targets and measure descriptions.
- Trade-offs (speed, context fragmentation, duplicates): 900/120 produces fewer fragmented snippets but larger chunks can include extra surrounding text; 600/80 offers finer granularity but can miss full statement context.
